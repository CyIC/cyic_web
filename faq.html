How do I join?

Membership is dependent on an applicant attending two meetings and successfully submitting a Stock Checklist for consideration. After submitting a stock checklist, a unanimous vote of the partners is required to accept an applicant.

Is this club subject to SEC regulations?

SEC has specific rules for investment clubs. Because of these rules, we are not open to the public for investment, and members need to be active in evaluating and voting on proposed securities.
